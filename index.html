Balthazar Auguste-Dormeuil is an architect based in Paris and Tel Aviv. He studied architecture in Kyoto Seika University and ENSA Paris Malaquais, where he graduated with Highest Honors in 2019.

He currently teaches a first year studio at the Negev School of Architecture, a multidisciplinary architecture school that explores the context of the Negev/Naqab desert.
