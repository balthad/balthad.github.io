About
Balthazar Auguste-Dormeuil, M.Arch, is an architect based in Paris and Tel Aviv. He studied architecture at the Kyoto Seika University and ENSA Paris-Malaquais, where he graduated with Highest Honors. He has since collaborated with multiple architectural practices in Paris, London and Tel Aviv. Balthazar currently teaches as a Studio Master at the Negev School of Architecture, Beer Sheva, a recently opened architecture school that explores the context of the Negev/Naqab desert. He is regularly invited as an external critic to numerous architecture programs internationally.
