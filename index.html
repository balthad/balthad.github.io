Balthazar Auguste-Dormeuil
About Balthazar Auguste-Dormeuil (M.Arch) is an architect based in Paris and Tel Aviv. He studied architecture at Kyoto Seika University (JP) and ENSA Paris Malaquais (FR), where he graduated with Highest Honors. Balthazar currently teaches a first year studio at the Negev School of Architecture, Beer Sheva, a multidisciplinary architecture school that explores the context of the Negev/Naqab desert. He is regularly invited as an external critic to numerous architecture programs.
