Balthazar Auguste-Dormeuil
 Balthazar Auguste-Dormeuil (M.Arch HMONP) is an architect based in Paris and Tel Aviv. He studied architecture at the Kyoto Seika University and ENSA Paris-Malaquais, where he graduated with Highest Honors. He has since collaborated with different architectural practitices in Paris, London and Tel Aviv.

His practice is articulated around architectural design, research and publication, in collaboration with different practitioners and institutions. The collaborative nature of the projects, as well as their existence within multiple fields, reflects a necessity in tackling contemporary issues.



Balthazar currently teaches as a Studio Master at the Negev School of Architecture, Beer Sheva, a recently opened architecture school that explores the context of the Negev/Naqab desert. He is regularly invited as an external critic to numerous architecture programs internationally.
